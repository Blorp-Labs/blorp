When a user enables blur nsfw, any type of NSFW media embed should be
blurs until they click "Show NSFW" or click into a post, opening the
detail or lightbox view of the post. Within that session of using Blorp,
we remember what NSFW posts a user has clicked on. If someone direct links
you to the detail or lightbox view of a NSFW post, we require you click "Show NSFW"
instead of automatically unbluring it.

All post card variants reveal NSFW content when clicked. The NSFW badge in the
post byline provides enough affordance that it's clear the post is NSFW, even
in the extra compact ExtraSmallPostCard.

In addition to bluring media within a post, we also blur community icon and banner.
When you click into a post, we unblur the community icon in the sidebar and post byline
since you've concented to viewing the NSFW media.
